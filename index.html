Understanding PTSD

PTSD, or Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder, can happen after a person experiences something frightening or overwhelming. It may make someone feel unsafe, anxious, or trapped in painful memories, even when they are no longer in danger.

Supportive actions such as creating a calm environment, listening gently, breathing slowly, doing relaxing activities, and practicing mindfulness may help someone feel safer in the moment.

This experience is for awareness only and is not medical advice.
